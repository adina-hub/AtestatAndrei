Tokyo Highlights

Short Description

Tokyo, Japan's bustling capital, traces its roots back centuries, evolving from a humble fishing village into the vibrant metropolis it is today. Amidst modern skyscrapers, echoes of its storied past can be found in historic sites like the Imperial Palace, dating back to the Edo period, and the ancient Senso-ji Temple in Asakusa, offering glimpses into Tokyo's rich cultural heritage and enduring legacy.
